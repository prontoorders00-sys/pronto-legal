Privacy Policy - Pronto Orders

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Pronto Orders provides a WhatsApp-based ordering and quotation assistant for wholesale businesses in South Africa.

Information we process

WhatsApp messages sent to a Pronto-powered business number (order lists and questions), your WhatsApp phone number and name, and order details generated from your messages.

How we use it

To understand order requests, reply with quotations, and pass confirmed orders to the business you are ordering from. We do not sell personal information and do not send unrelated marketing.

Service providers

Messages are transmitted via the WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta), hosted on Railway, and interpreted with the Google Gemini API.

Your rights (POPIA)

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by emailing prontoorders00@gmail.com.
